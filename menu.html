Our Menu

Our menu celebrates the very best of local produce, sourced from our country's most respected farmers and artisans. Each day a seasonal showstopper pays homage to the delicious diversity of cooking with fire - a dish you simply have to try.

Awarded chef David Higgs' quintessential South African fare, with his finishings, flavour and finesse, ensures that there is something for all good food lovers at our resturant.
